About Us

Our Mission

The purpose of Theta Tau is to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among its members, and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.

Theta Tau is the oldest, largest, and foremost Fraternity for Engineers. Since its founding at the University of Minnesota in 1904, over 35,000 have been initiated. With emphasis on quality and a strong fraternal bond, the Fraternity has chapters only at ABET accredited schools.
Theta Tau stresses the importance of professional ethics and exemplary academics and practices in engineering professions. Each chapter fosters a brotherhood that works cooperatively to create men who are leaders in the Fraternity, the community, on campus, and in everyday life; and also forges friendships between members.

Chapter History

Sigma chapter was founded on November 29th, 1924 as the 18th chapter of the Fraternity. Since then, nearly 1,000 brothers have been initiated into our chapter.
