Who we are

Sigma chapter was founded at The Ohio State University on November 29th,
1924 as the 18th chapter of the Theta Tau fraternity.
Over the years we have initiated almost 1,000 Brothers.
Today, we continue the traditions of those before us by recruiting
the most outstanding engineers.
